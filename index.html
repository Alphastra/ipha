About Us

Qwaze is an innovative educational platform designed to empower teachers and educators by streamlining the quiz creation process through advanced AI technology. Teachers can upload their course materials, and our AI model automatically generates customizable quizzes tailored to their content, saving valuable time and effort. The platform offers flexibility, allowing educators to define the number of quiz questions, and provides students with an intuitive interface to take quizzes directly on the platform or download them as Google Sheets for offline use.

Qwaze also delivers insightful analytics to help educators track student performance and improve learning outcomes. By blending cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design, Qwizup aims to enhance the educational experience for both teachers and students.

solution

Upload

The first step in the QWAZE process is to upload the course materials and documents provided by the educators. This can be done by simply dragging and dropping the files into the QWAZE platform or by uploading them manually. The platform supports various file formats, including PDF, Word, and PowerPoint, to ensure that educators can upload their materials in the format that is most convenient for them.

Generate

Once the course materials are uploaded, the QWAZE LLM engine takes over to generate the quiz questions. The AI uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to analyze the uploaded documents and identify relevant information, such as key terms, concepts, and definitions. The AI then generates quiz questions based on this information, ensuring that the questions are accurate, relevant, and engaging for the students.

Customize

After the quiz questions are generated, educators can customize them to fit their specific needs. This includes the ability to edit the questions, add or remove questions, and adjust the difficulty level of the quiz. The customization options allow educators to tailor the quiz to their students' needs and learning styles, ensuring that the quiz is both challenging and engaging.

Share

Finally, educators share the quizzes with students via the platform directely or have them as a google form with. Qwaze ensures quizzes are accessible and provides tools for tracking student performance, enhancing the overall learning experience.
